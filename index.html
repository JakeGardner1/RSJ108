How to Be a Sports Journalist

Assembly time: 1 week or longer
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you looking to start a professional career in sports? Sports Journalism is for you! This tutorial will teach you the tips and tricks on how to be a sports journalist and teach you ways to get a career regarding sports journalism.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Computer
  • Paper
  • Pen/Pencil

  1. Go to a sporting event.

    Going to a game will get you that experience of being at a sporting event and studying it to know more about that sport in general.

  2. Start to Write About your Experiences with that Sport.

    After you understand what the sport is, take some time to get a pen and paper or open a computer document, and write about what you saw.

    Writing about what you understood/saw in that sport will prepare you for your future career as a sports journalist.

  3. Look into Different Jobs Portraying to your Sport Around your Region.

    Once you are ready to apply, you should be confident with your sport and know that is the sport you want to cover.

  4. 4. Schedule an Interview with your Position.

    Before your scheduled interview time, take some time to get ready. Make sure that you present yourself in a professional manner.
